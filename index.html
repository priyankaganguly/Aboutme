About Me

Hello everyone! Welcome to my website. My name is Priya. I am a first year PhD student in the Rhetoric and Writing program at Virginia Tech (VT). This is my first semeester here at VT. I recently finished my MS in technical communication from Missouri University of Science and Technology (Missouri S&T). I also earned a master of science in botany from Calcutta University. At VT, I am working as a graduate teaching assistant in the engineering communciation program (ECP) at the Materials Science and Engineering deapartment.

At Missouri S&T, I also worked as a graduate teaching assistant (GTA). I taught technical writing course to the undergraduate engineering and science senior and junior students. Last summer (2019), I worked as a technical communications intern at Shure Incorporated where I worked on the content management system, XINA. Before coming to Missouri S&T, I worked as a human resource executive, client executive, and customer service executive in India, Canada, and the United States.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of digital and medical rhetoric (rhetoric of health and medicine) and technical and professional communication. I am also interested in exploring how rhetoric works in the academic writing genre.
