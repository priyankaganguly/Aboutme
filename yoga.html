I love yoga

Hello everyone. Here is a few reasons I love yoga.





  1. I love the peace I get.
  2. I love the power to work on myself and change my reactions to something.
  3. I love that I’m not clumsy anymore, and I even have the occasional moment of grace.
  4. I love the core strength and toned muscles that make me feel panther strong.
  5. I love that now that I’m trained in it, I can give myself a class anywhere, anytime. On the dirt while camping, rooftops, airports, public gardens, downtown L.A. Portable, baby!
  6. I love the ethical foundation it gives to my life.
  7. I love the way it helps me manage my energy and mood.
  8. I love that now I do less harm.
  9. I love to stretch creaky muscles when waking.
  10. I love that it’s taken away chronic neck/shoulder pain!
  11. I love that it shows me the truth.
  12. I love that I can’t imagine my life without it now.
  13. I love that it changes people.
  14. I love that it helps me be healthier.
  15. I love that it lowers heart rate and stress level.
  16. I love that it’s free now I can rock a class for myself, anytime I want!
  17. I love that it helps me lose weight and keep it off!
  18. I love that it makes me feel like falling in love.
  19. I love that I feel like a carefree kid when I do Happy Baby and Child’s Pose!
  20. I love that yoga can always be modified to fit people’s needs.
  21. I love how it is, and so isn’t, a fad.
  22. I love how it roots me deep to my culture of origin.

Maintaining a regular yoga practice can provide physical and mental health benefits

The benefits of yoga include the following:

  • increased flexibilty
  • increased muscle strength and tone
  • improved respiration, energy, and vitality
  • weight reduction
  • improved atheletic peformance
  • protection from injury
  • improved stress management
  • reduced headaches, drug abuse, and sleeping problems
  • increased body awareness
