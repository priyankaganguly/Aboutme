I love cooking

Hello everyone. Let me share with you the 5 reasons I love cooking.

It's fun!

Cooking gives me a chance to

  • be creative
  • indulge in some play time

It's delicious!

When you're cooking you get to chhose the following:

  • what you make,
  • what goes into each dish, and
  • the quality of the ingredients.

So it's easy to make things that are the most delicious to you.

It's good for your body!

The more I learn about nutrition the more I’ve come to realize that the most important factor for healthy eating is to

  • avoid processed food
  • focus on using real ingredients

And what is the best way to do this? Cook for yourself as much as you can.

It's good for the soul!

Spending 30 minutes in the kitchen preparing dinner will put me in a much calmer, less frazzled mood than sitting on the couch for the same amount of time.

The praise!

As much as I’d like to think that all these lofty benefits were what first got me into cooking, really, it was the praise.
